KIT KAT Makes Summer Sweeter With New Limited-Edition Churro Flavor

  • As if KIT KAT wasn’t already perfectly sweet, their latest flavor innovation is sure to make you show off your pearly sweet tooth. Starting next month, the new KIT KAT Churro flavor will be available nationwide for a limited time. It’s one of those unlikely “Why didn’t I think of this?” pairings that perfectly complement each other.
  • Inspired by summertime outings, to capture the true churro taste, buttery, churro-flavored creme with sugar was spread between KIT KAT’s iconic wafers. You can expect to see these popping up nationwide on June 6, which appropriately happens to be National Churro Day. Standard, king, and snack sizes will be available for a limited-time only.
